# Product Dossier – Ashwagandha Tablets

_Internal reference for content, marketing, and AI agents._

## Basic Info

- **Product name:** Ashwagandha Stress Balance Tablets
- **Category:** Stress & sleep support
- **Format:** Tablets
- **Key herb:** Ashwagandha (Withania somnifera) root extract

## Traditional Positioning

In Ayurveda, Ashwagandha is traditionally used to:

- Support the body’s ability to adapt to stress
- Promote calmness and emotional balance
- Support strength and stamina
- Help maintain restful sleep

Our positioning:
> “Daily support for stress resilience and restful sleep, inspired by a classical adaptogenic herb.”

We avoid:
- Claims like “cures anxiety” or “treats insomnia”.
- Overpromising timelines for effects.

---

## Key Messages for Content

1. **Stress resilience, not sedation**
   - Emphasise adaptation, steadiness, and recovery.
   - Avoid implying that tablets replace rest, boundaries, or therapy.

2. **Evening & nervous system themes**
   - Pair with routines around winding down:
     - Reduced screens before bed
     - Calming evening rituals (light stretches, reading, breath awareness)

3. **Audience framing**
   - People experiencing:
     - Busy, high-demand schedules
     - Occasional restlessness at night
     - Feeling “wired and tired”

Always phrase as support and maintenance.

---

## Safety & Precautions (Content Version)

- Not recommended for:
  - Pregnant individuals without personalised professional advice
  - People with known allergy to Ashwagandha or related plants

- People with thyroid disorders, autoimmune conditions, or on long-term medication should consult their healthcare provider before use.

Standard footer:

> “This product is based on traditional Ayurvedic use of Ashwagandha for stress and sleep support. It is not a substitute for medical treatment or mental health care.”
